### Slide 1: Welcome to RCS App
- **What you can do**: Send clear instructions, receive updates, and track progress.
- **Who it’s for**: Employees, Supervisors, Admins, System Admins.
- **Outcome**: Fewer missed tasks, faster responses, shared visibility.

### Slide 2: Mission (Why we’re here)
- **Act faster** with clear, timely instructions.
- **Be accountable** with read tracking and replies.
- **Stay notified** on web, email, and Telegram.

### Slide 3: Vision (Where we’re going)
- **One place** to see all actionable instructions.
- **Real-time collaboration** that’s simple and mobile-ready.
- **Measurable progress** without chasing people.

### Slide 4: Why this exists
- Email/IM are noisy; **action gets lost**.
- It’s hard to know **who read and who replied**.
- **Deadlines slip** without reminders.
- Leaders need **simple visibility**.

### Slide 5: Roles at a glance
- **Employees**: Receive, read, reply, and complete.
- **Supervisors**: Send, follow up, forward, and coordinate.
- **Admins**: See all instructions; monitor activity and reports.
- **System Admins**: Oversee system logs and settings.

### Slide 6: Features you’ll use most
- **Send instructions**: To people, roles, or everyone.
- **Deadlines**: Optional, clear due times.
- **Replies**: Keep context in one place.
- **Forward**: Add the right people fast.
- **Read tracking & timeline**: Know what happened, when.
- **Notifications**: In-app, web push, email, Telegram.
- **Dashboards & reports**: See progress by role.
- **Profile**: Update info and preferences.

### Slide 7: Quick start checklist
1) **Create**: Add title, details, optional deadline.
2) **Choose recipients**: Specific users, by role, or all users.
3) **Send**: Auto-notifies recipients immediately.
4) **Reply**: Share status and next steps.
5) **Forward**: Include more people when needed.
6) **Track**: Use timeline, read status, and dashboards.

### Slide 8: Getting started
- **Log in** to your account.
- Go to **Dashboard** for your updates.
- **Allow notifications** when asked — Required for instant browser alerts (web push).
- **Link Telegram** from your Profile — Required for real‑time Telegram alerts.

### Slide 9: Dashboard (What you’ll see)
- **Recent instructions** (sent/received) and quick stats.
- **Upcoming items** and recent replies.
- Supervisors/Admins: **Wider coverage** (team-wide or system-wide).

### Slide 10: Receive an instruction
- Go to: Sidebar → Instructions → **Received**.
- Open to see: **From**, **To**, **Deadline**, and **Body**.
- **Read status** updates automatically when opened.

### Slide 11: Reply clearly (use this template)
- Use the **Reply** box on the page.
- Template: “Status: In Progress. Blocker: Waiting for file. Next step: Upload by 3 PM.”
- Replies show instantly in the **Activity Feed**.

### Slide 12: Forward responsibly
- Click **Forward** on the instruction page.
- Add recipients and optional note.
- New recipients are notified and appear in the timeline.
- Tip: Forward only to people who need to act.

### Slide 13: Create and send
- Go to: Sidebar → Instructions → **New Instruction**.
- Fill: **Title**, **Body**, optional **Deadline**.
- Recipients:
  - **Specific users**
  - **By role** (Employees, Supervisors, Admins)
  - **All users** (excludes System Admins)
- Click **Send** to notify instantly.

### Slide 14: Notifications (Never miss updates)
- For best and real‑time notifications, complete both steps below:
- **Web push (Required for instant browser alerts)**: Allow notifications in your browser.
- **Telegram (Required for real‑time Telegram alerts)**: Link your account in Profile.
- **In‑app**: Bell icon → open items directly.
- **Email**: Get summaries in your inbox.

### Slide 15: Your profile
- Update **name, avatar, contact** details.
- Change **password** anytime.
- **Link Telegram (Required)** for real‑time Telegram alerts and toggle notifications.

### Slide 16: For Admins / System Admins
- **Monitoring**: View all instructions and activity.
- **Reports**: Read/unread, forwarded, monthly trends.
- **User management**: Manage users and activity logs.
- **System settings** (System Admin): Pending approvals, mail/system config.

### Slide 17: Safety & privacy
- **Role-based access**: See only what you should.
- **Secure sign‑in** with email and password.
- **Reliable delivery**: Multiple channels ensure reach.

### Slide 18: Tips for success
- **Use clear titles** and add a deadline.
- **Reply with status**: Done / In Progress / Blocked.
- **Forward thoughtfully** to only necessary people.
- **Enable notifications** to stay current.

### Slide 19: FAQs
- **Send to everyone?** Yes — choose “All Users”.
- **Who sees replies?** Sender and all recipients.
- **Do System Admins receive?** No — they monitor only.
- **Print an instruction?** Yes — use **Print** on the page.
- **Do I need to link Telegram?** Yes — linking is required to receive real‑time Telegram alerts. Also allow browser notifications for instant in‑app alerts.

### Slide 20: Help & support
- **Help**: Use the Help link in the app.
- **Contact Admin**: For access or role changes.
- **Stay updated**: Keep profile and notifications on.
